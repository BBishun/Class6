Unlike Pluto

Armond Arabshahi, known by his stage name Unlike Pluto, is an American singer, songwriter, DJ and record producer.
The name Unlike Pluto developed from holding onto his outcast persona. He is known for his Pluto Tapes series of albums,
where he challengees himself to release one song per week starting from May 2018.

Shadow is a song about being under someone's Shadow. It's about feeling small in this world, compared to other people.
I go through this all the time in the music industry. It takes a lot of courage, perseverance and self love to get through it!.
-Unlike Pluto, about his song "Shadow"
